About

What is Stuytinerary?

Stuytinerary is the official schedule application for students at Stuyvesant High School!

It was created in 2017 in Stuyvesant High School's Software Development class, taught by Mr. Dyrland-Weaver.

This application continues to be available thanks to the hard work of the Stuyvesant CS department, and the Stuyvesant Administration.

What Can You Use It For?

Stuytinerary offers a suite of functional applications to both students and faculty alike!

Check the bell schedule, whether it's an A or B day, log in and customize your home schedule to see which classes you have next... and more!
